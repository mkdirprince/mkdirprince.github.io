HNG12 Mexico - A Lesson in Communication

What did I do?

I mistakenly sent a message in the announcement-project Slack channel, which is strictly reserved for mentors to share task updates and stage assignments.

Why is this important?

HNG operates based on structured task completion. The announcement-project channel serves as a dedicated space for assignment communications. Messages from interns can clutter the channel, making it harder for others to locate important updates.

Lessons learned

I will carefully review my messages before sending and ensure they are directed to the appropriate channel or recipient.
